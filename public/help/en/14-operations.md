# Chapter 14 — Operations

## Overview

The Operations module tracks all field activities including agricultural operations, contracted services, and field progress.

## Tabs

### Operations Log
Record and view all field operations with details such as date, field, operation type, equipment used, and inputs applied.

### Contracted Services
Manage service contracts with external providers, track daily entries, and process payments.

### Field Progress
View a summary report of operations completed per field, showing coverage percentages and pending work.

### Input Usage
Track agricultural input consumption across fields and operations, with drill-down by product.

### Map
Visualize field boundaries, operation history, and equipment positions on an interactive map.
